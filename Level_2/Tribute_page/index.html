Actor Vivek
The King of Comedy
Celebrating the Life and Legacy of a Tamil Cinema Icon

Biography

Early Life and Career:

Vivekanandan, popularly known as Vivek, was born on November 19, 1961, in Kovilpatti, Tamil Nadu. He graduated from American College in Madurai and later pursued a master's degree in English literature. Vivek started his career in the film industry with minor roles but soon became known for his exceptional comedic talent and ability to convey social messages through humor.

Achievements

Environmental Activism with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam:

Actor Vivek joined hands with former President Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam to promote environmental conservation. Together, they launched the "Green Kalam" project, aiming to plant one crore (10 million) trees across Tamil Nadu. This movement was not only a testament to Vivek’s commitment to environmental causes but also a significant step towards a greener India. Their collaboration inspired many citizens to take part in tree-planting activities and raised awareness about the importance of preserving nature.

Receiving the Kalaimamani Award from Jayalalithaa:

In recognition of his outstanding contributions to Tamil cinema and his exceptional comedic talent, Vivek was honored with the prestigious Kalaimamani Award in 2006. This award, given by the Tamil Nadu government, was presented to him by the then Chief Minister, J. Jayalalithaa. The Kalaimamani Award is one of the highest honors in the Tamil arts community, and Vivek's receipt of this award marked a significant milestone in his illustrious career. It acknowledged not only his achievements in the film industry but also his efforts in promoting social messages through his work.

Receiving the Padma Shri Award:

In 2009, Vivek was conferred with the Padma Shri, the fourth highest civilian award in India, by the Government of India. This prestigious honor was in recognition of his immense contributions to the field of arts and entertainment. The Padma Shri award ceremony was a proud moment for Vivek, his family, and his fans. It recognized his exceptional talent, his dedication to using comedy as a tool for social change, and his impactful presence in Indian cinema.

Legacy

Even after his passing, Vivek's influence continues to be felt in the Tamil film industry. He remains an inspiration for new generations of comedians and actors who aspire to blend entertainment with meaningful messages. His legacy is carried forward by his fans, colleagues, and the many people he inspired through his work and his dedication to social causes.
